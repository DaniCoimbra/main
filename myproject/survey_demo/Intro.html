Thank you for participating in this session. It should take at most 30 minutes to complete, with 5 minutes allocated for this screen alone.

During this session, you will be asked to make a choice that will help us understand your attitude towards risk. The choice is a hypothetical investment of up to $100 in a lottery that has equal chances of increasing your investment by 30% or decreasing it by 10%. Any amount that is not invested in the lottery remains unchanged. Your choice will be used to calibrate a robo-investor, which will manage your investments for the duration of the session.

The session is conducted as a series of investment rounds. Each round represents a month of investment, and there are four blocks of four investment rounds. After each block, you'll have an opportunity to adjust your choice of hypothetical investment in the above lottery.

At the start of every investment round you will be given 100 token-dollars that the robo-investor will invest on your behalf. After each block of four investment rounds, you will be given the outcome of the investment, both cumulative and per round as well as an opportunity to change your hypothetical investment and thus re-calibrate the robo-advisor.

For your participation, you will receive the outcome of a randomly chosen investment block. The exchange rate between token-dollars and US dollars is 2 US cents for every 100 token-dollars. The average payment based on all possible decisions that a robo-advisor can take per investment block is $8.50 but you can make more or less depending on your hypothetical lottery investment and the outcomes of the investments chosen for payment. The absolute minimal and maximal payments are $4.60 and $17.30 respectively.

Good luck, and thank you for your participation!
