Thank you for participating in this session. It should take at most 30 minutes to complete.

During this session, you will be asked to make a choice that will help us understand your attitude towards risk. This choice will calibrate a robo-investor, which will manage your investments for the duration of the session.

You can revisit and adjust your choice every four investment rounds. Each round represents a month of investment, and there are four sections of four investment rounds. After each section, you'll have an opportunity to adjust your choice.

For your participation, you will receive $6, which is independent of your responses. Additionally, a bonus payment will be determined by a random draw of one of the four investment sections. Every investment period is equivalent to a month, and you will have $100 per month to invest. Your monthly allocation and the returns you earn will be yours in the end of the study session. Every fictitious dollar you earn is going to be equal to 1 US cent. On average your bonus will be $4.25 but it can be lower or higher than that depending on the investment decisions your robo-investor makes. The average above is taken over all possible decisions available to the robo-investor.

Good luck, and thank you for your participation!
