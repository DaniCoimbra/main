A robo-investor has been calibrated to make monthly investment choices on your behalf. The calibration was based on your choice of investment in the lottery on the previous screen. This lottery is only used to calibrate the robo-investor and will not be used for the determination of your bonus payment.

Scenario:

Every month you allocate $100 for your robo-investor to distribute between a risk-free savings account and a stock market index.

The robot is perfectly informed about the interest rate on the savings account and about the distribution of possible returns on the stock market index. Both the interest rate and the distribution of stock market index returns may change from month to month. Your robot will adapt choices to reflect your risk attitudes as inferred by your choice in the lottery of the last screen.
